Luxury Apartments in Pasadena, CA

Find a luxury studio, one, or two-bedroom apartment in the heart of Pasadena at Trio Apartments. It doesn’t matter if this is the first time you venture to live on your own, or if you’re in search of something new. We’ve updated our historical building to create a sophisticated, socially inspired, and comfortable living space with something for everyone.

Elegance is in the details, it’s found in curves and accents that you won’t find in your average apartment building. Trio brings together architectural creativity and urban sensibility. Our floor plans range from 532 to 1630 square feet, providing a variety of layouts to choose from. Designed with your comfort in mind, our pet-friendly apartments feature flat panel or barn doors with lever hardware, 9-foot ceilings with radius corners and oversized windows for you to enjoy. Large private balconies and patios make the space feel even larger, and the designer paint and lighting further enhance the clean-cut feeling of the apartment. Hourglass shaped bathtubs, walk-in closets, full size washer and dryer are just some of the perks you’ll enjoy at Trio Apartments.

With an almost perfect walk score, our prime location in Pasadena, CA, is close to Old Town and Lake Avenue, plus there are plenty of public transportation options nearby. Hollywood is less than half an hour away, and CA-110 freeway and US-101 give you an easy way in to or out of the city whenever you need it. Recharge and relax for a new day at Trio Apartments. Lounge by the pool, unwind on your private balcony, or enjoy an al fresco meal with your family and friends. When you're looking for a Pasadena luxury apartment, Trio Apartments has something for everyone. Come visit us today or give us a call and let’s get you in for a tour of the property!

Floor plans are artist’s rendering. All dimensions are approximate.  Actual product and specifications may vary in dimension or detail.  Not all features are available in every apartment.  Prices and availability are subject to change. Please see a representative for details.
